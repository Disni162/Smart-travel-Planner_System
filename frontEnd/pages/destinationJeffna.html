About the Destination

Experience the unique charm of the North. Jaffna is a land of colorful kovils, historic forts, and serene islands. From the sacred Nallur Kandaswamy Kovil to the breathtaking views of the Casuarina Beach, it offers a rich blend of culture, history, and natural beauty.
